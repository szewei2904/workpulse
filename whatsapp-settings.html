AI task & performance analysis
What WorkPulse does automatically once connected
📋
Pending task detection
Scans @mentions and directives in the group to identify tasks assigned to each staff member, and flags ones with no completion reply.
✅
Rounding & attendance tracking
Detects "Rounding Facilities Checklist" and "Daily attendance report" messages to calculate compliance per staff, per day.
⚠️
Facility issue & complaint flagging
Identifies messages mentioning broken, leaking, faulty, or pending repairs — categorised by area (pool, toilet, gym, IT, etc).
📤
Auto-generated daily group report
Every morning at 8:00 AM, a consolidated report with pending tasks per staff is generated, ready to send back to the group.
These features require an active WhatsApp connection above. Once connected, new messages sync automatically — no manual chat export needed.
